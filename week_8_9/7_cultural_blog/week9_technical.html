Chefs in the kitchen

May 5th, 2014

My first impression of DBC experience was 'competition'. By nature of programming school, I thought it was going to be intense and competitive, and people will not help you if you are lagged behind.
But now I see DBC from a different perspective. I see it more as a good community not as a competitive programming school. This is because I came to talk to members and staffs and got a sense of what it will be like to experience DBC.
I would like to engage myself in the way that I see it as home, where I can relax myself and feel safe. That is the environment I can make the most progress.
